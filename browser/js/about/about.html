Tearget began with a woman who desired to possess the tears of a goat. Sadly she quickly discovered that goats do not have tear ducts. She decided she would have to settle for the tears of a man instead. Soon all of her friends and acquaintances were begging her for tears. She invited a few friends along for the adventure and Tearget was born. We have quickly amassed a large selection of quality artisans who willingly supply their tears.
